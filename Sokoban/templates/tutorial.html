Sokoban Tutorial

Sokoban is a puzzle game where the player has to push boxes to their designated locations. The player can only push the boxes and not pull them. The player can also not push more than one box at a time. The game is won when all boxes are in their designated locations.

Controls

W, A, S, D to move

Objective

You want to push all the boxes to the goals!
